Help Center

Answers to common questions about Alsi

What is Alsi?

Alsi is an AI chat assistant built for Indian users. Fast, smart answers — right in your browser.

How many free messages do I get?

You get 20 free messages per day. Resets every 24 hours.

What are the subscription plans?

₹399/month, ₹999/quarter, or ₹3,999/year. All plans give unlimited messages.

Is UPI supported?

Yes! Payments via Razorpay — supports UPI, cards, net banking, and wallets.

Is my chat history saved?

Yes, your conversations are saved automatically.

Can I use voice input?

Yes, tap the mic icon in the chat to speak your message.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Cancel anytime from account settings. Access continues till end of billing period.
